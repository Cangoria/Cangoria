# Cangoria

> Early Chinese medicine · Medical texts · Formula structure · Pulse methods · Classical acupuncture

## English

This profile is a place for notes, source materials, and digital projects related to the history and literature of Chinese medicine. Much of the material discussed here comes from medical texts predating the Song dynasty, particularly those concerning formulas, materia medica, pulse methods, and classical acupuncture.

The projects introduced below are being developed collaboratively by our research team.

### Team research

- Textual study of 《醫心方》, 《千金方》, and 《外臺秘要方》, together with an analysis of their underlying formula structures
- Review of research on the pre-Tang origins of selected medicinal substances
- Organization of medicinal indications recorded before the Tang dynasty, with 《證類本草》 serving as a central reference
- Development of herbal treatment frameworks for common internal diseases
- Analysis of texts related to Bian Que pulse methods
- Exploration of pulse-acupuncture techniques through Chinese and Japanese medical literature
- Experimental work on the relationship between specific diseases, clinical specialties, formulas, and medicinal substances
- Study of classical acupuncture, traumatological techniques, and structural approaches to acupuncture
- Historical and technical study of special forms of vessel needling found in classical sources

I also practise acupuncture and herbal medicine, and this clinical experience offers another perspective from which to read historical descriptions of diagnosis and treatment.

### Reading and references

Across medical history, Han–Tang formula traditions, and classical acupuncture, the scholars and authors frequently consulted include 山田慶兒、真柳誠、李伯聰、憶忘（王庶）、廖育群、應志鵬、李建民、黃龍祥、陳曉輝、顧漫、范家偉.

這裡主要用來整理中國醫學史與醫史文獻方面的讀書筆記、原始資料及數位研究。所處理的材料多見於宋以前醫籍，內容涉及方劑、本草、脈法與古典針灸。

以下均為我們團隊目前正在進行或逐步推動的研究方向。

### 團隊研究方向

- 《醫心方》《千金方》《外臺秘要方》的文獻整理與方劑隱性結構分析
- 部分本草藥物唐以前基原考證成果的整理
- 以《證類本草》為中心，整理唐以前的藥物主治記載
- 常見內科疾病湯藥診療方案的建構
- 扁鵲脈法相關文獻分析
- 結合中日醫學文獻，探索脈針的技術路徑
- 專病、專科、專方與專藥的可行性實驗
- 古典針法、傷科手法、傷科針灸與結構針灸研究
- 古典刺脈法中特殊類型的歷史文獻與技術規範研究

此外，我也從事針灸與方藥臨床。這段經驗有時也能提供另一個角度，幫助我理解醫籍中的診療記載。
